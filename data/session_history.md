# Session History

## 2026-04-26 — Day 1 / Day 2 transition

### Topics covered

- Personal introduction
- City and place of living
- Work routine
- Transport to work
- Free time
- Sport
- Travel: mountains and sea

### Main repeated errors

- `mój/moja/moje` agreement: *mój miasto* → **moje miasto**
- Locative after `w`: *w średnie miasto* → **w średnim mieście**
- Transport: *idę autobusem* → **jadę autobusem**
- Reflexive verbs: *relaksować* → **relaksować się**
- Spelling: **siódmej**, **śniadanie**, **książkę**, **gotuję**, **zwykle**

### Current assessment

The learner has useful topic structure and enough vocabulary for a basic B1-style monologue, but endings, spelling, and verb forms are unstable. Next sessions should force short controlled production, not passive reading.
